MICRO SCALP

What is a Micro Scalp?

In simple words, micro scalping means injecting
pigment in the surface layer of the scalp.
Micro scalping is a method without side effects,
pain, or bleeding to thicken your hair.

Repetition on questions

Who is micro scalp suitable for?

This method is suitable for all ages and any gender

How long does it last?

Depending on the way of maintenance and the colorability of the scalp, it lasts for about 3 to 4 years

Is micro scalp the same as tattoo?

The only similarity between these two methods is in the injection of pigment into the scalp, and they differ in the material used, the angle and pressure of the hand, and the depth of penetration of the needle.

Is there pain and bleeding?

This method is completely pain-free, bleeding and recovery period.

Organic dyes such as Gost are derived from plants, are lead-free, contain anti-inflammatory and anti-allergic ingredients, and are compatible with all skin types. If used in the right combination, they prevent color change and fading.

The quality of the needle alloy plays an important role in the quality of performance.

The stroke of the microscalp device should be 4.2mm or higher. With this high stroke, the force of the needle hitting the scalp during operation also increases. How to control the device, the amount of hand pressure, and the depth of penetration of the needle into the skin require skill.
