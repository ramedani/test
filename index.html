What is a Micro Scalp?

In simple words, micro scalping means injecting
pigment in the surface layer of the scalp.
Micro scalping is a method without side effects,
pain, or bleeding to thicken your hair.

Who is micro scalp suitable for?

This method is suitable for all ages and any gender

How long does it last?

Depending on the way of maintenance and the colorability of the scalp, it lasts for about 3 to 4 years

Is micro scalp the same as tattoo?

The only similarity between these two methods is in the injection of pigment into the scalp, and they differ in the material used, the angle and pressure of the hand, and the depth of penetration of the needle.
